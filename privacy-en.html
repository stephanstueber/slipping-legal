2. General information

Protecting your personal data is important to us. We process personal data only in accordance with the applicable data protection laws.

Personal data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

3. Provision of the app

The “Slipping” app provides information about boat slipways and allows registered users to submit their own content, such as photos or suggested changes.

4. Registration and user account

Certain features of the app require registration of a user account. In this context, we process in particular the following data:

  • e-mail address
  • optionally, a name provided by the user

The processing is carried out for the purpose of creating and managing the user account and providing features that require registration.

5. Authentication and technical service providers

We use Supabase for authentication, database services, and file storage. The primary database is operated in the Central EU (Frankfurt) region.

Personal data is processed by these technical service providers only to the extent necessary for operating the app.

6. Location data

The app may use the device’s location data in order to provide location-based features, such as showing content near the user.

Location data is currently not stored permanently.

7. Photos and user-generated content

Registered users may upload content to the app, in particular photos and factual information relating to slipways.

This content is stored, processed, reviewed as part of the intended moderation processes, and displayed within the app once approved.

Uploaded content may be associated with the respective user account, the affected entry or slipway, and the processing or moderation status to the extent necessary for provision, review, abuse prevention, and traceability.

For uploaded photos, embedded technical metadata of the original file, in particular EXIF data such as location information, date taken, or camera information, is removed before upload and is generally not stored.

Users should nevertheless upload only images whose publication is permitted and in which no unwanted personal, sensitive, or otherwise protected information is visible in the image itself.

8. Review and publication

Submitted content is not automatically publicly visible. Publication takes place only after review and approval.

Approved content, in particular photos and factual information relating to slipways, may continue to be displayed within the app as content-related contributions to the platform even if the associated user account is later deleted, unless statutory or other legitimate reasons require deletion.

9. Server logs and technical processing

When operating the app, technically necessary data is processed in order to ensure the security, stability, and functionality of the app. This may include server logs and technical request data in particular.

10. Storage period

We store personal data only for as long as necessary for the respective purposes.

  • account data generally until the user account is deleted
  • technical log data only for the period required in each case
  • moderation and assignment information only for as long as necessary for operation, review, abuse prevention, and traceability
  • approved content may be retained even after an account has been deleted

11. Your rights

Within the scope of the law, you have in particular the right to:

  • access
  • rectification
  • erasure
  • restriction of processing
  • data portability
  • object to processing
  • withdraw consent previously given with effect for the future
  • lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority
